Past Paper Viewer

A dedicated viewing environment for past exam papers.

Download Now

Use past papers quickly and easily.

Switch between question papers, marking schemes, and supplementary sheets at the press of a button. Configure your workspace - view documents together, individually, or at different sizes.

For both teachers and pupils.

Use the Past Paper Viewer in the classroom to speed up lessons by gaining convenient access to the relevant documents without the need to manage individual files.

Use your own papers.

Integrate your own example or mock papers for use within the viewing environment.

How to Use

1. Download a Course Package

Download one of the available course packages from here.

2. Import the Course Package into PPV

Press the "Load Course Package" button and locate your downloaded Course Package.

3. Select a paper to view.

Press the newly imported subject and follow the steps to choose which paper you would like to view within PPV.
